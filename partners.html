Fix8 has joined forces with the these companies to create an official partnership program. This partnership aims to build a community around Fix8 and these firms will jointly work to promote and support Fix8 globally.




  MultiWave develops and maintains sophisticated trading / market data solutions for Japanese retail & institutional customers and gets frequently involved in FIX Protocol implementations and consulting work. MultiWave also has operations in Papua New Guinea since 2011 via its subsidiary, MultiWave PNG, which provides IT solutions to government, educational and business customers in the local market.




  Fix8 Market Tech (Fix8MT) develops and maintains the Fix8 open source Framework, and the commercially supported edition, Fix8Pro. Fix8MT has developers in Australia, China and the Russian Federation. Fix8MT is responsible for providing and managing additional support and consultancy services, and works closely with the Fix8 open source community to grow commercial support services through 3rd party ISVs.

  FIXSOL (Fidel Softech Pvt. Ltd) provides services and solutions based on the FIX Protocol to lower the cost of FIX Adoption and related operations and usage. FIXSOL helps the electronic trading community right from FIX Training for professionals to implementation and maintenance.

NINTEI is FIXSOL's flagship product.
NINTEI is an Automated FIX testing Tool designed with ease of use in mind. Users can define required Test suites and easily perform testing and simulations with orderly, efficient integration and operation with other elements in the system with no modification or conversion required. The product key tasks are to handle unit testing, Quality Assurance (QA) / Regression testing, Certification and Post Deployment Maintenance and at every potential stage of development.
