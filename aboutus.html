We are a small dedicated team of developers and IT specialists in the finance sector. We developed Fix8 and decided to make it available to everybody.

It is the view of the core Fix8 team to encourage and promote participation in the project. We are always looking for new members to join us.



David L. Dight

Project Lead

With more than 20 years experience in the finance sector David has developed numerous frameworks, messaging layers and middleware pieces. Over those years, while developing exchange system projects and in architectural roles primarily on the sell-side, David developed a keen interest and expertise in FIX. Having worked with many FIX frameworks, David was inspired to create his own in 2010 when he conceptualized Fix8 and began development.




Kristian Peacocke

Configuration Management
Atlassian Expert

Experienced in ITIL, Configuration Management, Change Management, Release Management, Incident Management, JIRA, Confluence, and Bamboo, Kristian's real enthusiasm is to manage people and business needs with happy and productive results. Kristian is continually tinkering with delivery processes to ensure the end result is enjoyable to live with and efficient to participate in - he gets a genuine kick out of making things run better! Highly regarded professionally, currently working at senior levels in IT management Kristian still fits in time for multi-platform video gaming, surfing, and other fun creative things!
